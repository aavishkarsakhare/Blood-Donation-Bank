🏥 Blood Donation Portal - Complete Work Showcase

This portal represents our commitment to leveraging technology for social impact. Below is a summary of all features we have built as part of this blood donation ecosystem:

✅ Donor Registration & Authentication: Secure signup/login system with profile management, allowing donors to register, update personal details, and track donation history.
✅ Blood Bag Inventory Management: Each donor can add blood units with automatic expiry tracking. Real-time calculation of available units across blood groups.
✅ Emergency Blood Request System: Hospitals and individuals can request specific blood types. The system automatically matches and allocates available units from registered donors.
✅ Interactive Blood Bank Directory: Map-based interface showing 10+ prominent blood banks across major Indian cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, etc.) with contact details and stock information.
✅ Blood Donation Camps Calendar: Upcoming camp listings across India with event registration functionality.
✅ Educational Resource Hub: Comprehensive blood donation education covering eligibility, health benefits, safety protocols, blood type compatibility, and myth vs facts.
✅ Donor Testimonials Gallery: 20+ real-life inspiring stories from donors across India.
✅ FAQ Knowledge Base: 20 frequently asked questions with interactive expandable answers.
✅ Admin Control Panel: Complete donor directory management, request tracking, and system oversight.
✅ Analytics Dashboard: Visual representation of donation trends using Chart.js showing donor growth and activity.
✅ Responsive & Accessible UI: Mobile-friendly design with smooth navigation across all modules.
✅ Indian Geographical Context: All blood banks, camps, and content tailored specifically for India with local phone numbers and regional addresses.

    📚 Comprehensive Blood Donation Education

    Knowledge saves lives. Learn everything about safe blood donation, health benefits, eligibility, and post-donation care.

    🩸 Who Can Donate?

    Age 18-65 years, weight ≥50kg, hemoglobin ≥12.5 g/dL. Generally healthy with no major illnesses.

    💪 Health Benefits

    Regular donation reduces iron overload, lowers risk of heart disease, burns 650 calories.

    ⏳ Donation Interval

    Whole blood: every 56 days. Platelets: every 7 days. Plasma: every 28 days.

    🍎 Pre-Donation Tips

    Eat iron-rich foods, stay hydrated, get good sleep, avoid fatty meals.

    🛡️ Safety Protocols

    All equipment is sterile & single-use. No risk of contracting diseases.

    ❤️ Post-Donation Care

    Rest for 15 mins, drink fluids, avoid heavy lifting for 5 hours.

    🌍 Global Impact

    Every 2 seconds someone needs blood. Your donation = 3 lives.

    🧬 Blood Types & Compatibility

    O- is universal red cell donor. AB+ is universal plasma donor.

    🏥 Myths vs Facts

    Myth: Donating blood weakens you. Fact: Body replenishes within 24-48 hrs.

    🌟 Rare Blood Types

    Rh-null (golden blood), Duffy-negative — only 1 in 6 million.
